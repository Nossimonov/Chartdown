# The Sundered Reach

**Status: aspirational** — the #73 stress test in progress. A two-continent world torn by an ancient cataclysm: the archipelago between the landmasses scatters along the trend line of a once-shared mountain range (the Spine of Aum west, the Broken Spine east), with realistic watersheds draining both. Six nations: Valemark and Carrowen (human), Ghor Vakh (orcish, walled off behind its Teeth), Khundrum (dwarven heights), Sathriss (lizardfolk swamps), and Ilvarien (the elvish isles).

Already forced out of the renderer: polygon seas and island landmasses (#76 — the half-plane water model drowned both continents on first render), water→realm→terrain paint ordering, lake-vs-sea layering, and viewport-clamped ocean labels.
