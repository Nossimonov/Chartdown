# NNNN — Title stating the decision, not the topic

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by [NNNN](NNNN-slug.md)
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What forces are at play? What problem demands a decision? Two or three paragraphs, written so a newcomer understands why this couldn't be deferred.

## Decision

The decision, stated in the active voice: "Chartdown uses …", "The parser will …". Include a worked example if the decision is about syntax.

## Alternatives considered

Each alternative with the honest reason it lost — including "do nothing."

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what future decisions this constrains. Include the losses; an ADR with no downsides wasn't examined hard enough.
